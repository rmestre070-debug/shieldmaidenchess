About Shield Maiden Chess

Shield Maiden Chess is a modern expansion of classical chess, designed to deepen strategic play while preserving the clarity and discipline of the original game.

Rules Overview

The board is 10×8. All classical pieces move normally. Shield Maidens move one square any direction but cannot check or be checked.

How to Play

Shield Maiden Chess plays closely to classical chess but with a wider board and new structural patterns.

How to Play — Visual Guide

Board layout, Shield Maiden placement, expanded center, and flank dynamics.

Opening Guide for New Players

Control the expanded center, use the Shield Maiden early, castle early, develop with purpose.
